Welcome to RubyRealm

Founded in 2024, RubyRealm began as a dream to make self-expression and creativity accessible through unique, affordable and quality products.

From handcrafted DIY kits to the latest accessories, every item we curate is made to empower and inspire.

Our mission is simple: create an exciting, inclusive shopping experience that feels as personal as your passions. We are proudly made in India and thrive on community support, customer feedback, and a desire to be bold, different and real.
